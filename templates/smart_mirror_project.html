3D Printed Smart Mirror V1

Mirror Features

Our users will have the ability to interact with and control the mirror using hand gesture controls. The system will operate within a defined distance from the mirror, and I am currently working on finalizing this distance as I continue to develop the technology.

Our mirror can be mounted in either a horizontal or vertical orientation, and it will automatically adjust to the chosen position.

Our users will have the ability to give voice commands to control their home environment with just your voice. Connect to your favorite music streaming services, set reminders, check the weather, and so much more without ever lifting a finger.

Our Mirror is equipped with the latest open-AI technology, ChatGPT, allowing you to ask complex questions and receive more human-like responses than ever before. With our innovative technology, you can enjoy a personalized and interactive experience, tailored to your unique needs and preferences.

Experience immersive sound with our Mirror's advanced audio system. Equipped with two powerful 3-Watt speakers and a supporting sound system, our Mirror allows you to enjoy clear sound quality while engaging with your personalized assistant, playing music, or using supported applications

Our mirror with support Augment Reality Applications. Merging the digital world with the real. For example you could have a virtual animated overlay of a clock positioned perfectly on the opposite side of the room. by default there will be simple utility and entertainment applications.

Create the perfect ambiance with our Mirror's advanced ambient lighting. With the ability to play animations and fully customizable settings, our ambient lighting allows you to set the mood and enhance your overall experience.

The design phase

With a frame that is both functional and aesthetically pleasing. The frame is divided into three main sections - the Front, Mid, and Rear - each of which is carefully engineered for ease of manufacturing and assembly. The Front section of the frame features a lip designed to hold and lock a two-way mirror, creating a sleek and seamless appearance. The Mid section of the frame provides support for the monitor and houses the main electronics, ensuring a stable and secure installation. Finally, the Rear section of the frame houses the ambient lighting LEDS and provides structural support for the mirror, with mounting indicators for easy installation.
